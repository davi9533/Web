About BAYVIEW

The edacious can find themselves an indulgence worth seeking at the Bay View Restaurant, serving breakfast, lunch and dinner. The restaurant menu boasts of decadent indian delicacies, alongside international cuisines plated with finest ingredients inherent to the region. The locally produced tropical fruits and fares are a delight that can be savoured while waiting for tides to bring the stars out against the bustling skyline. Our chef and his team have redefined the interplay of various local and international delicacies that find their curtsy onto the hand painted plates designed in Toronto GTA.
